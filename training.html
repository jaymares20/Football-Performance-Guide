Football Training

Football players need a combination of strength, speed, conditioning, and agility to perform at their best. A balanced training program can help athletes develop skills that are important for success on the field.

Important Areas of Training

Strength Training

Strength training can help football players develop power and prepare their bodies for the physical demands of the game. Exercises may focus on both the upper and lower body.

Speed and Agility

Speed and agility training focuses on acceleration, quick changes of direction, balance, and movement. These skills are important for many different positions in football.

Conditioning

Conditioning helps athletes prepare for the repeated physical effort required throughout practices and games. Training should include appropriate rest and recovery.

Sample Weekly Training Focus

Example of a general weekly football training focus
Day Training Focus
Monday Strength Training
Tuesday Speed and Agility
Wednesday Recovery
Thursday Strength and Conditioning
